Principal's Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Visitors,

Welcome to Sir Harry Johnstone International School, where excellence meets opportunity! It is my pleasure to greet you as the principal of this dynamic institution, where we are committed to nurturing young minds, fostering creativity, and building a strong foundation for lifelong success.

At Sir Harry Johnstone International School, we believe that education goes beyond the classroom. Our dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and inclusive learning environment empower students to discover their full potential. Whether in academics, arts, sports, or leadership, we encourage every learner to grow, explore, and achieve greatness.

As you navigate our website, we invite you to learn more about our programs, achievements, and the vibrant community that makes our school a home of excellence. Whether you are a prospective student, a parent, or an alumnus, you are always a valued part of our journey.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to welcoming you in person!


Principal, Sir Harry Johnstone International School
