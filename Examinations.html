Cambridge Checkpoint Information

Cambridge Checkpoint is part of Cambridge University, UK, operating as a non-profit department.

The exam structure has undergone changes, with a new system starting in 2024.

Exam Schedule

Day Subject Papers
Tuesday English Paper 1 & 2
Wednesday Mathematics Paper 1 & 2
Thursday Science Paper 1 & 2

Exams are sealed in the exam room and sent to the UK for marking.

Exam Fees

Level Fee per Subject
Primary Checkpoint MK 65,200
Secondary Checkpoint MK 88,500

Results will not be issued until all exam and school fees are cleared.
